Hermits

The hermits are a pretty small group that mean no harm to other players, all hermits just build and trade around the realm. The hermits collect resources so that they can get rich off of trading with other members from Sid and Ayush empire. Hermits also have a highway and a fee for not using the highway with a "Hermit Highway Pass." The fee costs 10 netherite blocks so make sure you always keep a Highway Pass on you. Hermits do not want to kill anyone and only want to collect, trade and build.
